Biography

Under construction. Some contents are samples of a website template.

Yosuke Shinya is a research engineer in computer vision and deep learning, especially in object detection and image generation. He is a project assistant manager at DENSO. For some projects, he writes papers and develops OSS as an independent researcher. He has received the Award of the Minister of State for Science and Technology Policy (at the 50th Japan Student Science Award), OpenMMLab Contributor of the Year Award 2021, and Papers with Code Top Contributor Award.
